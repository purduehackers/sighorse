FAQ

Q: What counts as an adventure?
A: Anything you're genuinely excited about exploring! Could be learning a new skill, building something, researching a topic, creating art, or embarking on a project that excites you.

Q: How long should my daily updates be?
A: As long or short as you want! A sentence about what you learned, a photo of your progress, or a git commit.

Q: What makes a good artifact?
A: Something that captures and shares your adventure in a way that others can access and appreciate. Think websites, videos, papers, documentation, art pieces, interactive demos or zines.

Q: How many adventures am I allowed to have?
A: No more than three — don't be greedy. As many as you want.

Q: Am I allowed to present a past adventure?
A: Yes.

Q: Do my adventure and artifact have to be distinct entities?
A: No; for example, writing a joke research paper is simultaneously adventuring while creating an artifact.
